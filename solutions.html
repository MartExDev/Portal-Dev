MartEx Launches New Ecosystem

MartEx is committed to developing a transaction and shopping platform that combines cryptocurrency and global conventional e-commerce, providing strong impetus for blockchain technology. Our ultimate goal is to provide infrastructure for blockchain, create value for its ecosystem, improve the problem of transaction settlement, and build a new model and world of mutual trust, security, openness, and sharing.

Decentralized e-commerce

MartEx is a decentralized e-commerce platform, providing users with an efficient bridge for transactions between major global traditional e-commerce platforms. Users can use cryptocurrency to purchase commodities from major e-commerce platforms (e.g. house, car, furniture, electronics, daily necessities, cosmetics, luxury goods, etc.), enabling them to buy goods from all over the world.

Network Roles

In MartEx's product line, there are three main user roles:

① Consumers: Crypto holders
② Brokers: Responsible for dealing with customer orders.
③ Sellers: Traditional e-commerce platforms.

Privacy Preserving

When users place an order with MartEx (MEX), public information is stored in the blockchain storage (IPFS, Arweave, etc.), with the key information and storage hash submitted to the smart contract. The encrypted private information (such as receiving information, etc.) is transmitted to the corresponding allocated node through SSL tunnel using P2P (Peer to Peer) technology. Upon receipt of the information, only the specific node can decrypt it with its own private key, combining the public data from the smart contract and blockchain storage to form a complete order. After the order is finished, all the sensitive information will be removed from the node itself.
