MartEx For Developers

Join a Rapidly growing developer community

MartEx is an open ecosystem. We welcome all technicians and experts with development experiences to join, contribute what you can to the community, make joint efforts to realize the goal of implementing a completely decentralized e-commerce platform (MartEx) as soon as possible.

What can I do?

MartEx system provides reserved easy interfaces for developers to connect. No matter what the capabilities of developers are, you can always find places where you can do in the MartEx network. Join us, develop and spread MartEx to the world.

Developer
Implementing local malls to serve local neighbourhoods.
Creating custom DApps for family and friends.
Developing additional smart contracts based on MartEx's.
Contributor
Testing smart contracts and participating in bug bounty programs.
Developing documentation and translating into multiple languages.
Developing tutorials and instructional videos to share the technology.
Ambassador
Actively participating in various activities organized by MartEx.
Volunteering in MartEx's developer community to help people, etc.
Promoting online and offline.

60,000,000 $MEX rewards

In MartEx's token metrics, 6% of total supply tokens are reserved for community developers for incentivize the developer community, for active contributors, developers and ambassadors, depending on the contributions you have done, you will receive corresponding tokens as rewards. For those developers who connects local popular malls to MartEx, they will receive a certain proportion of the transaction share using that implementation additionally.
