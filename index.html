Welcome to MartEx

Get your favorite products with your crypto digital assets seamlessly.

MartEx is a decentralized e-commerce platform that provides users a bridge between the crypto world and major traditional e-commerce platforms. It allows users to buy products online with their cryptocurrencies without hassle.

How MartEx Works

MartEx's mission is to bridge the gap between the crypto market and traditional e-commerce market and build a secure and seamless user trading experience.

MartEx Features

Users are able to securely and efficiently purchase products and services using their cryptocurrency, regardless of geographical location or currency

Cross-border Shopping
Shop without limits, anywhere and anytime, regardless of where the products are produced.
No Currency Restrictions
No need for a valid bank account to purchase goods from region-specific markets, simply use your crypto assets.
Low Rates
Enjoy the extremely low payment rate and efficient confirmation time of blockchain when buying global goods with MartEx.
Open Scalability
Developers are able to access MartEx's ecosystem through protocols, SDKs, and documentation, and receive corresponding rewards.
Privacy Preservation
No private information will ever be publicly saved. All is secured and only available to the broker who handles your transaction for a short period of time.
Security
MartEx takes different measures to make sure your funds are always safe. Thanks to the nature of blockchain, all transactions are known by nodes and cannot be denied.
Developer Experience
MartEx is creating a developer-friendly community to help grow the ecosystem. There will be protocols, SDKs, API docs, grants, hackathons, etc.
Modularity
High customizability, extensibility, upgradeability, short time-to-market, and community collaboration.
